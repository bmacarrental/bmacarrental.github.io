Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of car rental services tailored to your needs.

Airport Transfers

Hassle-free pickups and drop-offs to/from Kempegowda International Airport Bangalore (BLR). We offer fixed pricing for airport transfers, ensuring transparency and no surprises. Our drivers track your flight schedule to ensure timely service, even if your flight is delayed.

  • Fixed, all-inclusive pricing (mention exceptions like tolls if any).
  • Flight tracking for punctual pickups.
  • Meet and greet service available upon request.
  • Available 24/7.

City Disposals

Explore Bangalore at your own pace with our flexible hourly rental packages (City Disposal). Ideal for business meetings, shopping trips, sightseeing, or personal errands within city limits. Choose your car and duration (e.g., 4 hours/40 km, 8 hours/80 km).

  • Flexible packages based on hours and kilometers.
  • Professional drivers familiar with city routes.
  • Perfect for multi-stop journeys within Bangalore.
  • Available for Sedan, SUV, and Premium categories.

Outstation Trips

Planning a trip outside Bangalore? Our outstation car rental service is perfect for weekend getaways, family vacations, or business travel to other cities. We offer competitive per-kilometer pricing for round trips or multi-day bookings across South India and beyond.

  • Transparent pricing per kilometer (check our Pricing page).
  • Minimum kilometers per day applicable.
  • Driver allowance (bata), tolls, and state taxes are typically extra.
  • Wide range of vehicles including Sedans, SUVs, and Tempo Travellers.
  • Experienced drivers for long-distance travel.

Tour Packages

Discover the beauty of South India with our curated tour packages. We offer pre-planned itineraries covering popular destinations like Mysore, Ooty, Coorg, Wayanad, Tirupati, and more. Packages often include vehicle rental, driver, and sometimes accommodation suggestions.

  • Well-planned routes covering key attractions.
  • Customizable packages available upon request.
  • Ideal for family vacations and group travel.
  • Travel comfortably in our Sedans, SUVs, or Tempo Travellers.
  • Contact us to discuss popular routes and package details.
